How to Manage Cookies

If cookies aren’t enabled on your computer, your shopping experience on DYOM.com will be restricted to browsing and researching; you cannot add products to your basket and buy them.

To enable cookies:

If you aren’t sure what type or version of web browser you use to access the internet



For PCs: Click ‘Help’ at the top of your browser window; select ‘About’

For Macs: Click on the Apple menu of the open browser window; select ‘About’

How to Confirm Cookies are Enabled for PCs

Google Chrome:

Click on ‘Tools’ in your browser window. Select Options. Click ‘Under the hood’; locate ‘Privacy’ and choose ‘Content settings.’ Select ‘Allow local data to be set.’

MozillaFirefox:

Click ‘Tools in your browser window. Select the privacy icon. Click on Cookies; select ‘Allow sites to set cookies.’

Safari:

Clock on Cog icon atop your browser window; select ‘Preferences.’ Click on ‘Security’; select ‘Block third party and advertising cookies.’ Click ‘Save.’

How to Confirm Cookies are Enabled for Macs

Safari on OSX:

Click ‘Safari’ atop your browser window; select ‘Preferences.’ Pick ‘Security’ and then ‘Accept cookies.’ Select ‘Only from site you navigate to.’

Mozilla and Netscape on OSX:

Pick ‘Mozilla’ or ‘Netscape’ in your browser window; select ‘Preferences.’ Scroll down and find cookies under ‘Privacy and Security.’ Select ‘Enable cookies for the originating website only.’

Opera:

Click on ‘Menu’ atop your browser window; click on ‘Settings.’ Pick ‘Preferences and then select ‘Advanced’ tab; select ‘Accept cookies.’

All Other Browsers:

Please consult online help files or your documentation.

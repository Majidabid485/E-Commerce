FAQ's

How do I register or create an account?

To create an account or register with us, click ‘Login/Register’ at the top of the home page and fill in your details. Once you’re done, click ‘Save account.’

How do I sign in to my account?

To sign in to your account, click ‘Login/Register at the top of the home page and enter your email or username and password. Click ‘Login.’

What do I do if I forget my password?

Click on ‘Login/Register’ and scroll under the login section. Click on ‘Lost your password?’ and enter your email or username. Click reset password. You will receive a secure link from us detailing how to create a new password via email.

How do I update my account information?

Login in to your account to edit or change any information, including your email, password and stored addresses.

How do I update my shipping and billing address?

Login to your DYOM account. Click ‘My addresses’ and then select ‘View my addresses,’ which will show you your saved addresses. Select ‘Edit addresses’ and make the required changes. Be sure to click ‘Save’ after you are done or your data may be lost. Your updated addresses will now be saved for all future orders.

Where’s my order?

You may view your order(s) by signing in to your account on ‘Login/Register’ and clicking on the ‘Orders’ tab. This will show your completed orders and your current orders in progress; the latter can also show the stage of progress for your current orders. You may re-order old jobs using the same graphic files.

How do I pay for my order?

To expense your placed orders, you can pay through our trusted and secure online payment gateway options, Easy Paisa shop, Easy Paisa Mobile Account, or even pay via credit/debit card. All payments must be made on the day we process your order; failure to do so may result in a time delay or cancellation of the order. Cash on Delivery is also available through Leopard Courier service.
